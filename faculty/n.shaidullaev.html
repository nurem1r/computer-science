Professional Journey & Leadership

Before joining IAAU, Nurlan Shaidullaev served as an Informatics teacher and distinguished himself as a Republic Olympiad and International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI) trainer at the prestigious Kyrgyz-Turkish Boys High-School named after Ch. Aytmatov.

As one of the two founding directors of the Research and Development Center at IAAU, he has been instrumental in shaping the research culture and academic excellence of the institution.

Currently leading the Computer Science Department, he combines his extensive teaching experience with innovative leadership to advance computer science education and research.
